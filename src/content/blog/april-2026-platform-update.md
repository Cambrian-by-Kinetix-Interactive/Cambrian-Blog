We've been building fast. Here's what's new on Cambrian this month and what it means for creators and buyers.

## Creator Profiles Are Live

This is the biggest update. Every creator on Cambrian now has a dedicated profile page \u2014 your own storefront where buyers can browse your full catalog, see your licensing terms, and come back when they need more.

Your profile shows:

- **Your catalog** \u2014 every track you've listed, organized and searchable
- **Your bio** \u2014 who you are and what kind of music you make
- **Your licensing defaults** \u2014 so buyers know what to expect before they click

Think of it like your own shop inside the marketplace. Buyers who license one track and like it can now easily find everything else you've made. This is how repeat buyers happen.

If you haven't set up your profile yet, head to your [creator dashboard](https://cambrianmusic.com) and fill it in. A complete profile builds trust and makes buyers more likely to purchase.

## Improved License Selection

We simplified how creators set licensing on each track. You now choose from three clear paths per track:

- **Non-exclusive** \u2014 sell to many buyers, keep ownership
- **Exclusive** \u2014 one buyer only, higher price
- **Buyout** \u2014 full ownership transfer, highest price

Each path has its own price field. You can offer all three or just one \u2014 it's up to you. The new interface makes it faster to set up and clearer for buyers to understand what they're purchasing.

## Track Analytics

You can now see how your tracks are performing. The new analytics dashboard shows:

- **Views** \u2014 how many times each track has been seen by buyers
- **Licensing activity** \u2014 which tracks are generating interest and conversions
- **Search impressions** \u2014 what buyers searched for when they found your music

This data helps you make better decisions about what to create next. If your ambient tracks are getting 5x more views than your EDM tracks, that's useful information. Let the market guide your production.

## Smart Search Improvements

On the buyer side, we've improved how search works. Buyers can now filter by:

- **Mood** \u2014 tense, uplifting, cinematic, energetic, calm
- **Use case** \u2014 YouTube, podcast, game, ad, film
- **Tempo range** \u2014 find tracks that match specific pacing needs
- **License type** \u2014 filter by what's available (non-exclusive, exclusive, buyout)

Better search means better matches, which means more licensing for creators who tag their music properly. If you haven't revisited your track metadata recently, now is a good time.

## Faster Checkout

We cut the purchase flow down to fewer steps. Buyers can now go from finding a track to downloading a licensed copy in under 30 seconds. License terms are displayed clearly on every track page \u2014 no PDFs, no email chains, no ambiguity.

## What's Coming Next

We're working on a few things we're not ready to announce yet, but here's what's on the near-term roadmap:

- **Batch upload** \u2014 list multiple tracks at once instead of one by one
- **Collections** \u2014 group tracks into themed sets that buyers can browse
- **Payout improvements** \u2014 faster and more transparent payment processing

We ship updates regularly. If you want to stay in the loop, subscribe to this blog or follow us on social.
